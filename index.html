Our Story

Over 25 years ago, Clay and Debra Pierson started teaching martial arts out of a small trailer in Brooklet, Georgia. What began as a simple way to give back to the community has grown into an organization serving multiple cities across Coastal Georgia.

Master Clay Pierson's training lineage runs through Grandmaster Mark Cox, Grandmaster Dennis Ichikawa (head of the American Tang Soo Do Alliance), and connects to legends like Chuck Norris and Pat Johnson — the man who choreographed fight scenes in The Karate Kid and Enter the Dragon.

In addition to Tang Soo Do, our curriculum integrates disciplines from Kyusho Jutsu (under Grandmaster Jack Hogan), Combat Hapkido, Krav Maga, and Progressive Arnis through our organizational partners at Hogan Karate International and Modern Arnis International.

The entire Pierson family holds master-level ranks or above in American Tang Soo Do, along with various ranks in other arts. We've produced quality black belts for over two decades, and we're just getting started.
